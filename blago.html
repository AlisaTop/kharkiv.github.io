Blagoveshchensky cathedral

CATHEDRALS & CHURCHES

The Cathedral of the Annunciation of the Blessed Virgin Mary is the largest in size Cathedral of Eastern Europe of the Kharkov-Bogodukhov Diocese of the Ukrainian Orthodox Church of the Moscow Patriarchate.

The first church in the name of the Annunciation of the Blessed Virgin Mary was founded around 1655.
