Freedom square

One of the biggest squares in Europe

Sightseeing is one of the most important stages of arrival in any new city. In Kharkov, it begins with the local Freedom Square. Kharkov Freedom Square is the largest in Ukraine, the sixth in Europe and the twelfth in the world. Its area is incomplete 12 hectares, and the height difference is more than 11 meters.

Various events and exhibitions are often held on the square. For example, exhibitions of military equipment, products of Kharkov production, etc. Concerts, fairs, holiday demonstrations are held here. If necessary, temporary scenes, pavilions and stands are erected.

  • Under the square there are two metro stations at once: Gosprom and Universitet
