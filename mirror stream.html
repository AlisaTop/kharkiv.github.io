Fountain & Arbor

Arbor and fountain in Kharkov, the first symbol of the city. It is located on Sumskaya Street in Victory Square, opposite subsequently (in the 1980s) the newly built new building of the Kharkov Opera and Ballet Theater. The glass jet was built in 1947 according to the project of the architect V. I. Korzh .



Over the past years, the gazebo has become the most recognizable and first generally accepted official symbol of Kharkov.
