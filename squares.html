SQUARES

Squares in Kharkiv

Square «Arrow» is situated at the confluence of the rivers Lopan and Kharkiv, where, according to legend, the city of Kharkiv began. A couple of years ago Square has been restored and in the warm season it is one of the most popular destinations of Kharkovites and city guests. From the points of attraction in the park are located the boat station and a new pedestrian bridge over the river Kharkiv, who in two years of existence has become a traditional venue for wedding photo shoots. Theatralnyi or Theatrical Square was founded in 1876 on the site of the old ramparts 2nd line and today is a pedestrian transit zone between the streets Sumska and Pushkinska. At both ends of the square set monuments – a bronze bust of Alexander Pushkin closer to Pushkinska street, on the other hand, face to Shevchenko theater is bust of Gogol. Since the moment of founding Kharkiv and before war the place of the square was occupied by several buildings – first there was the famous Kharkov Collegium, where famous philosopher Grigorii Skovoroda taught young people. Then, after the building of the Collegium was demolished due to its dilapidated, was built shopping mall – Old Passage, destroyed during the war. By its end it was decided to arrange the terraced park, there was also built a cascade of fountains. After the recent reconstruction park began a new life – people are going out, enjoying the magnificent sunsets and coolness of fountains, and on Fridays and weekends here are musicians playing.
