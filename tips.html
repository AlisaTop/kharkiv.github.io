Tips for tourists

Main page / Tips for tourist

Learn phrases in Ukrainian

Learn a few phrases in Ukrainian, this gesture will certainly be appreciated.

E-Ticket

Buy an electronic ticket upon arrival. It applies to all types of transport.

Cash

Have cash with you. In some places you can not pay by card.

Walking

Walk more on foot around the city. Especially in the evening there is a beautiful view of the night city.

Visit excursions

It is more profitable to visit some excursions in the company, in this case you can save significantly.

Bike rental

Rent a bike. There are many bike paths in Kharkov. So you can see a lot more.
